Education and Empowerment

Improving access to education for girls and women is a cornerstone of promoting gender equality. This can be achieved through:

  • Ensuring equal opportunities for girls to attend school.
  • Providing scholarships and financial aid to support girls' education.
  • Tackling cultural barriers that prevent girls from accessing education.
  • Creating safe and welcoming school environments for girls.
  • Training teachers to address gender stereotypes.
