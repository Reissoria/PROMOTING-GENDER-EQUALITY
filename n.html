WHAT IS WAYANG KULIT?

Wayang kulit, also known as shadow puppetry, is a traditional Indonesian art form that combines storytelling, music, and puppetry. It's a captivating performance where puppeteers manipulate intricately carved leather puppets behind a screen, creating shadows that tell stories from Hindu epics like the Ramayana and Mahabharata.

The puppets are illuminated by a lamp, casting their shadows onto the screen, and the puppeteer's voice brings the characters to life. The performance is accompanied by a gamelan orchestra, providing a rich musical backdrop. Wayang kulit is not just entertainment; it's a cultural treasure that reflects Indonesian history, mythology, and values.
